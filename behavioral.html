STAR answer guide

StepWhat to say
S — SituationGive context in 1–2 lines. Mention project, team or challenge.
T — TaskClarify your responsibility and expected result.
A — ActionExplain what you did personally, how you debugged or improved outcome.
R — ResultQuantify impact: timing closure, area drop, bug fix, schedule gain, validation success.

How to explain projects

  • State the problem first.
  • Explain architecture at block level.
  • Highlight exactly what you owned.
  • Describe trade-offs and bugs faced.
  • Close with validation and measurable outcome.

Typical follow-up questions

  • Why did you choose this architecture?
  • What failed first during bring-up or simulation?
  • How did you validate corner cases?
  • What would you improve in v2?
  • How did your work interact with firmware, layout, or test?

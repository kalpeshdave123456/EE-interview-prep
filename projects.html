Project explanation checklist

  • Problem statement and why it mattered
  • Your exact contribution vs team contribution
  • Architecture / block diagram explanation
  • Design trade-offs and constraints
  • Verification method and observed bugs
  • Measured result or expected silicon/system impact

High-value follow-up questions

  • What bottleneck did you face and how did you isolate it?
  • What would you redesign if area or power became critical?
  • How did you validate correctness beyond normal simulation?
  • What assumptions did your design rely on?
  • How would the design scale for higher frequency?

Strong project discussion template

SectionRecommended content
ProblemOne clear sentence on what the design solves.
ArchitectureTop blocks, interfaces, clock/reset scheme, dataflow.
ImplementationRTL/analog blocks, choices made, design trade-offs.
VerificationTestbench, coverage, assertions, corner cases or lab validation.
ResultsTiming, throughput, power, area, stability or performance impact.
LearningsWhat broke, what improved, what you would change next.
